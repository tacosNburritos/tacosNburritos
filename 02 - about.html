NewJeans is a South Korean girl group that debuted in July 2022 under ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation. The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They quickly gained attention for their fresh and nostalgic concept, blending elements of Y2K fashion with a modern twist. Their debut single, "Attention," became an instant hit, showcasing their youthful energy and catchy melodies. NewJeans' music and visuals have been praised for their unique approach, setting them apart in the competitive K-pop industry.
